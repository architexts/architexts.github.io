What are some features of LACY?

Code Tours

The Codebase Tour feature simplifies navigating large codebases with tailored exploratory and guided tours. Exploratory tours focus on understanding existing features, while guided tours, curated by experts, highlight key components and their relationships. This ensures all stakeholders, technical or non-technical, can efficiently interact with the codebase and gain relevant insights.

Quizzes

At the end of each tour take the quizzes to enhance your understanding. If you do not understand the context during a tour step you can ask questions or write notes.

Podcast

You can also listen to the tour like a podcast. Our system explains the codebase in a natural, conversational style—guiding you through files, logic, and design choices just like a senior developer would. It's a hands-free way to explore and understand complex projects.

Dashboard

Codebase experts can monitor the progress of the tours and quizzes. They can also see the questions asked by the users and answer them. This way they can understand which parts of the codebase are not clear to the users.
